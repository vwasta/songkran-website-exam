Songkran Celebrations

Songkran is one of the most exciting festivals in Thailand. It is celebrated with water fights, parades, and cultural activities. Check out the key locations below for Songkran celebrations:

  • Bangkok: Silom and Khao San Road
  • Chiang Mai: Old City Moat area
  • Pattaya: Beach Road
